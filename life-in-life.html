Life in Life

Conway's Game of Life at two scales. Each meta-cell is a 12×12 inner Life simulation isolated by dead-cell walls. The outer Life rules determine which meta-cells live or die — when a meta-cell is born it gets a random seed; when it dies its inner world is erased. Click any meta-cell to toggle it.
